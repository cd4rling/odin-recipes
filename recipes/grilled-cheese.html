Yummy Grilled Cheese

Description

This hearty, vegetable lasagna is the only lasagna my husband will eat. We love it! Hope you all enjoy it as much as we do.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • 2 Tbsp of butter or margarine

Steps

  1. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat
  2. Place bread slices in melted butter and turn 90 degrees
  3. Place cheese slices on bread
  4. Cook until cheese is melted
